About this space

Hoots Treehouse is a picture-perfect, romantic, luxurious treehouse with all mod cons in an area of outstanding natural beauty - only 45 minutes south of M25. A wonderful place to chill-out and lose yourself - you won't want to leave! Sheer bliss!
Guest access
Hoots has its own dedicated parking area. From here there is a lit boardwalk with steps that lead directly into the treehouse (about 50m). There is a tennis court that can be used (however please bring your own racquets and balls!)
Other things to note
The treehouse is a wonderful place to visit in all seasons - enjoy a gas lit bbq on the beautiful decking in the warmer months, or "snuggle up" in a wonderful comfortable sofa next to the wood-burner during the colder months
